Frequently Asked Questions

How can I adopt a dog?

Fill out the application on our "Adopt Now" page. Our team will review it and contact you within 48 hours for an interview.

Is there an adoption fee?

No, we do not charge an adoption fee. However, donations are always welcome to help us cover medical and food costs.

Are the Success Stories real?

Yes, every story is 100% authentic, shared by the loving families who adopted their furry friends from our shelter.

What do volunteers do?

Volunteers help with bathing, walking, and feeding the dogs, as well as assisting with cleaning and shelter maintenance.

How are my donations used?

100% of your donations go directly toward medical treatments, high-quality food, and improving the shelter's living conditions.

Can I visit the shelter?

Of course! You are welcome to visit us during our visiting hours (9 AM - 5 PM) at the location listed on our Contact Us page.
